Rallies

Our Club holds two rallies per month, on the 2nd and 4th Sunday of the month. There are also regular mid-week rallies and occasional twilight rallies in the warmer months. Members also post impromptu invitations for social rides. General information, rallies and events is posted on facebook.


Grounds

Set in the Morning Mist Reserve, Langley Road, Cranbourne South the grounds are shared with Cranbourne Pony Club. There are five arenas, three in regular use and two smaller arenas. We also have a cross country jumps course through the bush tracks and the club house is a modern facility.

HRCAV

We are a member of the HRCAV and follow their guidelines for riding gear and activities, our uniform is a royal blue and white or red. Membership aligns to the HRCAV year 1st of November through to the 31st of October.

Ride with us!

Riders can book and pay for rallies online via the Event Secretary web application. Rallies offer a mix of group and semi-private sessions in flat, poles, show jumping, cross country or 3 phase equitation. Groups are of mixed ability of 2-6 riders depending on the type of activity.

Events

The club hosts a couple of events each year for fun and to raise funds for the club. Members contribute to the running of the club by helping at events, working bees and with setting up and packing away on rally days.
